Resolving Medical Data Silos

⚠️
The Problem

Records are scattered across clinics, so patients can't carry a complete history between providers. And using AI on those records normally means uploading raw personal health data to a central server — a leak and compliance risk.

💡
Our Solution

AnamPouch encrypts every record in your browser before it leaves the device. Sensitive details are redacted before any AI summary, the encrypted file is stored on decentralised Walrus, and its fingerprint is anchored on Sui — so you hold the key, not a platform.

What You Can Do

🔑
Passwordless Sign-In

Sign in with Google via zkLogin. A non-custodial Sui wallet is derived for you automatically — no seed phrase, no browser extension, no Web3 friction.

🎙️
Voice & Document Intake

Capture a visit by voice, or snap a prescription or lab report. Speech recognition and OCR turn it into a clean, structured record in seconds.

🩺
AI Summaries, Privacy-Preserved

An AI drafts a concise clinical summary — but sensitive personal details are redacted before anything reaches the model, so your raw data never leaves your control.

📊
Health Dashboard

An at-a-glance view of your current status, active medications and recent records — every entry carrying an on-chain integrity badge you can trust.

🐋
Encrypted Records on Walrus

Reports and images are encrypted in your browser and stored on decentralised Walrus, with their fingerprint anchored on Sui — tamper-evident and always portable.

🔐
Revocable QR Sharing

Share a record with a doctor via a time-boxed QR grant. The doctor needs no wallet and pays no gas, and you can revoke access on-chain the instant you choose.
