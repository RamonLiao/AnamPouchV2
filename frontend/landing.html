Resolving Medical Data Silos

⚠️
The Problem

Fragmented data silos prevent patients from maintaining a unified, continuous medical history. Traditional cloud AI processing risks exposing extremely sensitive personal clinical records to central servers.

💡
Our Solution

AnamPouch locks medical records locally on your personal device. Utilizing secure edge AI for auto-summaries and blockchain for decentralized verification, we eliminate central data leaks once and for all.

Hybrid Architecture

🧠
On-Device AI

Lightweight, specialized local speech-to-text (ASR) and large language models (LLM) run offline on your device, processing consultations locally.

🛡️
Zero-Trust Storage

Your medical data is encrypted client-side using robust cryptographic keys, keeping records completely private and invisible to third parties.

⛓️
Blockchain Proofs

Tamper-proof cryptographic hashes are anchored directly to the Sui blockchain via OpenClaw, ensuring verified, immutability without revealing record contents.

⏳
Ephemeral Sharing

Share records securely with doctors using time-bound, secure QR codes. Relies on decentralized peer verification; strictly view-only with no downloads.
